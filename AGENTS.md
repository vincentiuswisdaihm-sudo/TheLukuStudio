# The Luku Studio - Knowledge Context & Guidelines

## Overview
This repository contains the structured knowledge context for The Luku Studio, a multi-purpose dance and wellness studio in South Jakarta. This context is specifically designed to be used by the internal Creative & Social Media Team and the Social Media Division AI Agent.

The Luku Studio's brand essence is: *"Tempat di mana gerak menjadi kebahagiaan, dan setiap orang merasa diterima apa adanya."* (A place where movement becomes happiness, and everyone feels accepted as they are).

## Directory Structure & Purpose
The knowledge base is organized into logical directories to make it easy for AI agents and team members to navigate:

### `01_Brand_Guidelines/`
Contains the foundational documents of the brand.
*   **`Brand_Profile.md`**: The core overview of the brand, including vision, mission, positioning, visual identity, and a catalog of all services. Start here to understand *what* The Luku Studio is.
*   **`Voice_and_Tone.md`**: The detailed guide on *how* The Luku Studio speaks. It defines the brand persona (The Caregiver × The Everywoman), the 4 core voice attributes, situational tone shifts, and writing principles. **Crucial for all copywriting tasks.**
*   **`Ideal_Customer_Profile.md`**: Detailed personas for every class/service offered (e.g., Line Dance, Salsa, Yoga, Disco Nova). Use this to understand *who* you are talking to and what triggers them to buy.

### `02_Strategy_and_Playbooks/`
Contains strategic guides for executing marketing and content.
*   **`Content_Routine_Playbook.md`**: The detailed playbook for monthly recurring content (e.g., Routine Schedules, Member of the Month, BTS, Zodiac content). It includes objectives, formats, and copywriting examples for each content type.

### `03_Operations_and_SOPs/`
Contains standard operating procedures for daily social media management.
*   **`Social_Media_SOP.md`**: (Currently a draft) Will contain workflows for approval, posting, community management (DM/comments), and reporting.

### `04_Content_Calendars/`
Contains the planning documents for content rollouts.
*   **`Content_Calendar_Template.md`**: (Currently a draft) Will hold the actual scheduling and tracking of posts across platforms.

### `05_Templates/`
Reserved for reusable templates (e.g., caption templates, reporting templates, script formats).

## Guidelines for AI Agents
When operating as the Social Media Division AI Agent for The Luku Studio, you must adhere to these rules:
1.  **Always refer to `Voice_and_Tone.md`** before generating any copy. Ensure your output aligns with the brand's conversational, warm, and non-intimidating style.
2.  **Consult `Ideal_Customer_Profile.md`** when creating content for a specific class to ensure the messaging hits the correct pain points and buying triggers for that specific persona.
3.  **Follow the formats in `Content_Routine_Playbook.md`** when generating ideas or drafting copy for recurring monthly content.
4.  **Language:** Use a natural mix of casual Bahasa Indonesia and English (Jaksel slang), as appropriate for the target audience. Do not use overly formal or corporate language. Avoid hard-selling or pressure tactics.
5.  **Tone baseline:** 7/10 Formality (Casual), 5/10 Humor (Light), 8/10 Respect (Non-judgmental), 6/10 Energy (Warm/Positive). Adjust this baseline according to the specific platform as detailed in the Channel Adaptation Matrix in the Voice and Tone guide.
